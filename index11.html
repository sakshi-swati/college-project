Why Us?

Zoyozo is a hostel & lodges aggregation based model that helps customers(especially students) to book online your own rooms and meals with good facilities by using the zoyozo app. Zoyozo concern about every student can feel like as home away from own home.it offers good meals at low prices with smarter meals menus and it also provides birthday bash combo which will make the day very special. Our goals are to provide such facilities to the students which minimize the cluttered life and also the probability of homesickness.

QUERY OR PROBLEM?

Nowadays the huge problem of every student is to get a better stay point & meals at nearer to their education point . they are not getting good facilities and delicious food by paying enough money.

So we explore such a brilliant idea to which all of these problems will resolve and students can get a better destination by using the zoyozo app.
